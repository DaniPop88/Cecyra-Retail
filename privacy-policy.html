Privacy Policy
At CECYRA RETAIL MARKET, we are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. We value your trust and aim to be transparent about how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our store, website, and services.
1. Information We Collect
We collect information that helps us serve you better, including:
  • Contact details such as your name, address, phone number, and email.
  • Order information, including items purchased and delivery instructions.
  • Payment details, processed securely by trusted third-party providers.
  • Communication records when you reach out to us for support or inquiries.
2. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used exclusively for purposes such as:
  • Processing your orders and arranging delivery or pickup.
  • Providing updates about your order status and store offerings.
  • Responding to your questions and requests for assistance.
  • Improving our services and tailoring your experience.
We do not sell or share your information with unrelated parties. Your privacy matters to us.
3. Data Security
We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data. Payment information is handled by secure payment providers, and we restrict access to your data to authorized team members only.
4. Your Choices
You may review or update your information anytime by contacting our team. If you wish to remove your data from our system, please let us know and we will process your request promptly.
5. Updates to Privacy Policy
We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The most current version will always be available on our website.
6. Contact Us
